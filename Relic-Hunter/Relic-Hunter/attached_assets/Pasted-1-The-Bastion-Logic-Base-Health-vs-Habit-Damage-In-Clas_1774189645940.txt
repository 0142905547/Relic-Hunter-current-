1. The "Bastion" Logic (Base Health vs. Habit Damage)
In Clash of Clans, other players raid you. In Relic Hunter, your Bad Habits raid you.
The Logic: Your Village has a "Fortification" score based on your Tank (Endurance) stats.
The Event: If you fail a "Hazard" (a daily habit like 'No Soda' or 'Sleep by 11 PM'), a Shadow Raid occurs.
The Calculation: * If your Walls are high level, the raid is deflected (No Gold lost).
If your Walls are low, the "Shadows" steal 10% of your banked Gold and "break" a building, pausing its passive production until you finish a "Repair Task."
2. The "Monument" Logic (Relic Synergy)
Relics shouldn't just sit in a bag; they should be the "Power Sources" for your buildings.
The Logic: Each Class Building (Forge, Sanctum, etc.) has Relic Slots.
The Interaction: * A Level 1 Forge has 1 slot. A Level 20 Forge has 5 slots.
If you slot the Obsidian Blade into the Forge, the Forge glows red and produces 2x Gold.
The "Discovery" Bonus: If you slot three Relics from the same "Set" (e.g., three Dragon-themed relics), you unlock a Hidden Skill Tree Branch that wasn't there before.
3. The "Fog of War" Logic (Exploration via Effort)
The map shouldn't be fully visible. You are a Hunter in a lost land.
The Logic: The base starts as just the Town Hall in a small clearing. Everything else is "Darkness."
The Clearing Mechanic: You don't buy land with money. You clear it with "Lantern Charges".
How to get Charges: You earn 1 Charge for every "Elite" task (Deep Work) completed.
The Reward: As you clear the Fog, you find Fixed Points of Interest (Pre-set ruins like an "Ancient Library" or "Cursed Well") that provide unique daily quests.
4. The "Inhabitant" Logic (The Citizen System)
Your base should feel populated by the "Souls" of your completed goals.
The Logic: Every time you reach Level 10 in a Class (e.g., Level 10 Mage), a Class NPC moves into your village.
The Librarian (Mage): Sells you "Scrolls" (One-time task boosters).
The Blacksmith (Warrior): Allows you to "Reforge" Relics to change their stats.
The Visual: If you are on a high streak, your village is full of NPCs walking around, music playing, and lights on. If you are slacking, the NPCs disappear, and the village looks like a ghost town.
5. The "Time-Dilated" Construction (Focus-Sync)
This links your real-world "Time" to the game's "Construction Time."
The Logic: Building a "Level 10 Sanctum" takes 48 hours.
The "Focus-Burn" Mechanic: If you start a Focus Timer (Pomodoro) in the app while a building is upgrading, the building finishes faster.
The Ratio: For every 1 minute of real-world "Deep Work," 5 minutes of "Construction Time" are shaved off.
The Result: You don't just wait for buildings to finish; you work them into existence.
